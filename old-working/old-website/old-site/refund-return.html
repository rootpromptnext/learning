Refund and Return Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2024

  • While we do not offer refunds for our courses, we provide a 1-day free demo for you to assess the course content and instructor style.
  • This allows you to make an informed decision before purchasing the full course. Once the course is purchased, it is considered final.
  • However, we understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise that prevent you from continuing with the course. In such cases, you may request to attend another batch of the same course, subject to availability.
  • Additionally, if you miss any topics during the course duration, you may request materials or recordings of those topics to catch up.
  • Similarly, for recorded videos, you can preview sample videos before making a purchase. Once purchased, refunds are not possible, but you can request materials or recordings of any missed content.
  • Please contact us to discuss your situation, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.
