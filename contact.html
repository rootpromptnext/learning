FAQ

What is the course duration?

The course runs for 60 working days (Monday to Friday).
Each session is 1.5 to 2 hours daily, ensuring consistent and focused learning.

What are the class timings?

Class timings depend on the batch.
Generally, sessions are conducted from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM (IST) on weekdays.

Is placement assistance available?

Yes, placement assistance is available.
Students are added to our ex-students professional group, where working professionals actively share opportunities and referrals. We are also working on partnerships with placement agencies to expand career support.

Do you provide a DevOps final project after course completion?

Yes, the final project is an integral part of the course. You will work on a real-world DevOps implementation that covers:

✔ Infrastructure as Code (IaC) – Create and provision infrastructure required for deploying the application using automation tools and pipelines.

✔ Application Deployment – Deploy the application on the created infrastructure using a complete CI/CD pipeline.

✔ DevSecOps Pipeline – Implement security checks and best practices within the pipeline to ensure secure and reliable deployments.

This project helps you gain hands-on experience with industry-style DevOps workflows and prepares you for real job scenarios.

Do you provide resume assistance?

Yes.
We provide sample industry-ready resumes and personally review your resume to ensure it aligns with current DevOps and Cloud job requirements.

Are mock interviews conducted?

Yes.
We conduct mock interview sessions to help you prepare for technical, scenario-based, and practical DevOps interview questions.
